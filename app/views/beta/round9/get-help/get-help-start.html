Get help searching

It costs an extra £3.50 for our team to search for you. This means one certificate costs £16 in total.

Certificates are sent out 15 working days after you make your order, so any certificates ordered today should be dispatched on Tuesday 5 August.

Before you start

You will need to check the details you entered in your search and provide the following additional details of the person you are looking for:

  • their first name, if you have not already told us
  • their date of birth
  • their place of birth
  • their mother's first and maiden names
  • their other parent's full name, if they would be listed on their birth certificate

If you are adopted, or searching on behalf of someone who is adopted, you do not need to tell us their parents' names.
