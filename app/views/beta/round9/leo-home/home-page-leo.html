Order a birth certificate

Before you start

You will need:

  • the year the birth was registered
  • their surname at birth
  • their sex at birth
  • a debit or credit card, to order a certificate

The details you provide should be as they would be written on official documents, such as a passport, marriage certificate or driving licence.

If the birth was registered in the last 50 years, you will also need to tell us:

  • their date of birth
  • their mother’s first and maiden names
  • their other parent’s full name
