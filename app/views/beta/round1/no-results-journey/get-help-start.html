Get help finding a record

It will cost an extra £3 to get help finding a record and will take an extra 3 weeks.

Before you start

You will need to check the details you have already entered in your search and provide the following additional details:

  • the person's age
  • the person's place of death or their last known address

If we cannot find your record

We may contact you for more details or to give you a partial refund if we cannot find a record that matches the details you provide. You would be refunded what you have paid minus an admin fee of £3.50.
