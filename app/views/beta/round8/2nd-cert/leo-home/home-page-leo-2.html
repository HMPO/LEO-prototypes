Order a birth certificate

Before you start

Searching

To search for a birth certificate, you will need:

  • the year their birth was registered, which may be different to the year they were born
  • their surname at birth
  • their sex at birth

The details you provide should be what is written on official documents, such as a passport, marriage certificate or driving licence.

Ordering

The details you provide should be:

  • their parents’ full names
  • their mother’s maiden name
  • a debit or credit card
