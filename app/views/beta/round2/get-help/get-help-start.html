Get help finding a record

It will cost an extra £3.50 to get help finding a record and will take 2 weeks longer.

Cost for one certificate Estimated delivery
Get help death certificate £16 Wednesday 5 March
Death certificate £12.50 Friday 21 February

Before you start

You will need to check the details you entered in your search and provide the following additional details of the person you are looking for:

  • their first name, if you have not already told us
  • how old they were when they died
  • the date they died
  • where they died or their last known address

If the person you are looking for was 16 or under when they died, you will also have to tell us:

  • the full names of their parents
  • your relationship to them

If we cannot find your record

You would be refunded what you pay minus an admin fee of £3.50 if we cannot find a record that matches the details you provide.

Go back if you want to search for a record yourself.
