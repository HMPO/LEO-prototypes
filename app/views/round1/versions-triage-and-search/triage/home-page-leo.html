Order a death certificate

Before you start

You will need the following details:

  • the year the death was registered
  • the person's surname at the time of death
  • the person's sex at the time of death

You can also tell us additonal, optional information to help with your search, for example if you know the GRO index reference.

What is a GRO index reference?

This is the unique reference the General Register Office (GRO) gives all births, deaths and marriages when they are registered in England or Wales.

It includes the year and district they were registered in.
