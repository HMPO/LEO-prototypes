Before you start

You will need:

  • the year the death was registered
  • the person's last name at the time of death
  • the person's sex at the time of death
  • a debit or credit card

The details you provide should be:

  • as they would be written on official documents, such as a passport, marriage certificate or driving licence
  • as they were when they died
